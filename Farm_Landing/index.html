How does
it work?

Agriplot® Due Diligence platform is designed to streamline and enhance due diligence processes. The platform facilitates effective and informed decision-making by managing and monitoring every step of the due diligence workflow. From initial screening of mill lists and tracing to the plot, to understanding legal compliance, conducting risk assessments, and developing risk mitigation strategies for high-risk areas, ensures a comprehensive and efficient approach.

Implement an
End-to-End Solution

Implement an end-to-end solution with our integrated system that oversees the entire journey from account creation to shipping registration. The platform facilitates insightful tracking of suppliers, trace-to-plot functionality, compliance analysis, risk scoring, and comprehensive risk mitigation strategies. Ensure seamless implementation in adherence to EUDR requirements, all within a single, efficient platform designed to elevate companies’ due diligence practices.
